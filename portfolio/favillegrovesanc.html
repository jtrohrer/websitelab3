A Walk Through Faville Grove Sanctuary

This map was a result of my Geography 370: Introduction to Cartography final project. We took aspects of all the cartographic information taught throughout the semester and were given creative freedom for map creation. I chose to map Faville Grove Sanctuary, a previous workplace for me, showing ecosystems, trails, and important locations to the sanctuary. Faville Grove was a place that I held close to me and got to learn quite a lot about, so I felt mapping would be a good test of my cartographic skills as well as what I had learned throughout my time there.
