A Bright Future

In this story map I walk through the timeline of coal and solar energy contributing to electricity in the United States. Providing background of the beginning of each energy form as well as why/how their use has shifted with time. Following that I discuss the United States' largest consumers of coal and solar energy and why these consumers likely still use those energy sources. I designed this to contrast the two energies and provide insight to their usage.
